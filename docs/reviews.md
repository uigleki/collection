## ğŸ® Games

### To the Moon

A memory journey embracing final moonlight vows

- **Reverse chronology reveals**: Deeply moving romance unveiled through fragmentary recollections. Effect shown before cause, making each revelation increasingly heartbreaking.
- **"For River" and everything after**: Theme song perfectly elevating emotional impact. Hauntingly melodic piano themes subtly remixed throughout, layering emotion upon emotion.
- **Symbolic imagery threading through**: Rabbits, moonlight, lighthouses. Simple mysteries concealing surprising, devastating revelations.

### What Remains of Edith Finch

Every room a theater of memory

- **Each tale through unique devices**: Every story presented through distinct narrative forms. Kaleidoscope of innovative approaches.
- **First-person fantastical journeys**: Experiencing impossible lives through your own eyes. Walking through others' moments as if your own.
- **Fantasy and reality fused**: No boundary between imagination and truth. Memory transformed into lived experience.

### Finding Paradise

A soul's journey toward perfect existence

- **Warm life contemplation**: Thoughtful examination of ideals, reality, and regret. What it means to pursue happiness and reconcile reality with fantasy.
- **The smallest moments matter most**: Story revealing mundane details as ultimately most significant. Characters flawed yet real, imagination woven with truth.
- **Mementos as prisms**: Each object imbued with myriad meanings. Viewing multi-faceted lifetimes through simple things, certain items creating profoundly moving moments.

### Steins;Gate

Fate's ultimate decree

- **Rigorous time travel mythology**: Complete and internally consistent scientific framework. Real-world science woven seamlessly into narrative.
- **Everything placed with purpose**: Masterful foreshadowing from opening to conclusion. Every detail meticulously positioned, all elements converging.
- **Salvation versus sacrifice**: Harrowing choices between saving one or another. The weight of altering fate bearing down on every decision.
- **Bonds across world lines**: Sincere connections deepening across timelines. Understanding forged through shared impossible experiences.

**Shortcomings**:

- **Slow initial pacing**: Extended slice-of-life foundation requiring patience through narrative setup.
- **Cryptic true ending requirements**: Key decision points designed with excessive subtlety, nearly demanding outside guidance.

### 7 å¹´å¾Œã§å¾…ã£ã¦ã‚‹

"I'll be waiting for you, 7 years from now"

- **Cross-temporal commitment**: A promise made seven years ago, crystallizing the most sincere feelings.
- **Story through recovered fragments**: Memory exploration gradually unveiling truth. Each chapter drawing you forward through carefully crafted revelations.
- **"LIFE" strikes profound resonance**: The resolution theme at the climax. When emotions truly hit.

**Shortcomings**:

- **Some forced narrative turns**: Excessive pursuit of suspense occasionally resulting in less organic plot developments.

### ASTLIBRA Revision

15 years to resurrect the golden age

- **Unpredictable narrative progression**: Altering the past through mystical scales produces dramatic, unexpected developments.
- **Outstanding musical experience**: Soundtrack perfectly complementing scenes, creating full immersion from combat to story.
- **Impactful climactic moments**: Key story beats filled with gravitas and emotional weight. Exploring fate, sacrifice, love, and the meaning of choices and regret.
- **Golden age RPG reborn**: Capturing pure joy of side-scrolling action JRPGs. Rekindling childhood wonder.

**Shortcomings**:

- **Grind-heavy with punishing difficulty**: Late-game requires substantial grinding and features punishing difficulty spikes.
